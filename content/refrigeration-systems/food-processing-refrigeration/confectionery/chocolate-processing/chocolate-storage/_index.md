## Components

- Storage Temperature 15 To 18c Optimal
- Temperature Range Acceptable 10 To 21c
- Relative Humidity Below 50 Percent
- Fat Bloom Temperature Fluctuation Defect
- Sugar Bloom Moisture Condensation
- Flavor Absorption Odors Packaging
- Shelf Life Dark Chocolate 12 Months
- Shelf Life Milk Chocolate 6 To 9 Months
