## Components

- Chlorinated Polyvinyl Chloride
- Temperature Rating 180 To 200f
- Pressure Rating 100 To 400 Psi
- Corrosion Resistant
- Scale Resistant
- Solvent Cement Joints
- Thermal Expansion Consideration
- Support Spacing 3 To 4 Ft
- Lower Cost Than Copper
